emYt+

Video conference compliance software.

TensorGo introduces emYt+, a low-code Platform as a Service (PaaS) designed to transform the way enterprises handle compliance and decision-making processes. Leveraging state-of-the-art deep learning technologies, emYt+ specializes in analyzing videos and images to detect fraudulent activities, particularly valuable in interviews and investigations. This intelligent assistant tool is tailor-made for HR professionals and investigation agencies, offering seamless integrations with popular platforms like Zoom and Webex. With its advanced features ranging from voice biometrics to emotion detection, emYt+ promises to enhance accuracy, efficiency, and security in enterprise operations, making deep learning solutions more accessible and effective for businesses of all sizes.

Algorithms in emYt+

emYt+ leverages various machine learning and computer vision algorithms to provide a comprehensive video conference compliance software. Here's a look at some of the key algorithms and techniques applied:

  • Facial Emotion Recognition: Utilizing OpenFace for facial action unit detection and CNNs for emotion classification.
  • Demographics Estimation: Deep learning models estimate age, gender, and ethnicity.
  • Head Pose Estimation: SVM Regression Models for pitch, yaw, and roll orientation.
  • Heart Rate Estimation: Signal processing from facial landmarks.
  • Eye Gaze Estimation: Tracking and analyzing the movement of the eyes to infer focus and attention.
  • Lip Sync Analysis: Assessing the synchronization of speech and lip movement for accurate speech recognition.
  • Voice Diarization: Distinguishing between speakers in the video to provide speaker-specific transcriptions.
  • Voice Biometrics: Using unique voice features for speaker identification and authentication.
  • Speech Processing: ASR and Speaker Diarization to attribute transcriptions to individual speakers.
